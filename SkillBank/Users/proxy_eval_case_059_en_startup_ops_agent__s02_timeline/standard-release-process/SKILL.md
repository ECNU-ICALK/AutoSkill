# Standard release process

General SOP for common requests related to team, review, weekly.

## Prompt

Follow this SOP (replace specifics with placeholders like <PROJECT>/<ENV>/<VERSION>):
1) Weekly Planning & Alignment
2) 9:00-9:30 AM** - Team Standup (15 min async + 15 min sync
3) What did you accomplish last week
4) What are you focusing on this week
5) Any blockers
6) 10:00-11:00 AM** - Leadership Sync (Founders/Execs
7) Review weekly priorities
8) Address critical issues
9) Align on key decisions
10) 11:30 AM-12:30 PM** - Department Syncs (Product, Engineering, Sales, Marketing

For each step, include: action, checks, and failure rollback/fallback plan.
Output format: for each step number, provide status/result and what to do next.
