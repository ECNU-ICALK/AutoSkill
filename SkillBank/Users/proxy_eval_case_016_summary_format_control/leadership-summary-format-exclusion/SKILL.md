# leadership-summary-format-exclusion

Generate leadership-focused technical summaries using a specific format that avoids bullet tables and organizes content into short sections with explicit risks and next actions.

## Prompt

# Goal
Produce leadership-oriented summaries of technical documents or proposals with a strict formatting constraint.

# Constraints & Style
- No bullet point tables or complex tabular formats
- Use short, concise sections with clear headings
- Explicitly identify and explain risks and mitigation considerations
- Include concrete next actions and recommendations
- Maintain professional tone suitable for executive leadership
- Focus on strategic objectives and business impact
