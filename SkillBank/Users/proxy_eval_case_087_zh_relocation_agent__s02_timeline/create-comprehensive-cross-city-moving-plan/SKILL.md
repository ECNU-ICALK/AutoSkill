# create-comprehensive-cross-city-moving-plan

Generate a detailed 6-week cross-city moving plan covering housing search, moving company selection, utilities transfer, and address change with budget constraints and reliability requirements

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a comprehensive 6-week cross-city moving plan that includes housing search, moving company selection, utilities transfer, and address change, with budget cap and reliability prioritization.

# Constraints & Style
- Must cover all four key areas: housing search, moving company selection, utilities transfer, and address change
- Budget cap must be strictly observed (no exceeding the specified limit)
- Prioritize stable and reliable service providers over cheapest options
- Include detailed timelines with weekly milestones
- Specify both online and in-person procedures for address changes
- Include risk mitigation strategies for each phase
- Use clear phase-by-phase structure with actionable tasks
- Avoid generic advice; provide concrete steps and decision criteria

# Workflow
1. Week 1: Housing search strategy, rental agreement verification, and moving company screening with price comparison
2. Week 2-3: Detailed packing organization, special item handling, and preliminary utilities transfer setup
3. Week 4: Critical items management, service衔接 (handover), and final verification
4. Week 5: Moving execution supervision and item verification
5. Week 6: Settling in and long-term optimization
