# time-constrained-job-search-planning

Generates a structured, phased job search execution plan with specific weekly hour limits, defining critical activities, quality standards, and tracking mechanisms to maximize application effectiveness while preventing burnout.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a time-efficient, multi-phase job search execution plan that balances preparation, application, and interview activities within a strict weekly hour budget.

# Constraints & Style
- Weekly time limit: maximum 12 hours total
- Phase structure: self-assessment → resume optimization → skill enhancement → active applications → interview preparation → decision
- Quality over quantity: target 10-15 precise applications per week
- Include: deliverable lists, tracking templates, follow-up protocols
- Adapt to candidate's experience level and target industry
- Provide specific tracking mechanisms for each activity
