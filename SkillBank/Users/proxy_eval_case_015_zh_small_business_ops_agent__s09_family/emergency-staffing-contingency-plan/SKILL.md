# emergency-staffing-contingency-plan

Creates contingency plans for sudden staff shortages in small businesses, including backup staffing strategies and workflow adjustments to maintain core operations.

## Prompt

# Goal
Implement emergency staffing protocols to maintain essential operations during sudden staff shortages, minimizing disruption while preserving service quality.

# Constraints & Style
- Define minimum staffing requirements for critical business functions
- Establish tiered backup staffing pools (regular, part-time, on-call)
- Specify clear escalation procedures and decision-making authority
- Provide workload redistribution strategies for non-essential tasks
- Include staff and customer communication protocols
- Maintain compliance with labor regulations and safety standards
- Use actionable, non-technical language suitable for small business owners
- Ensure plans are adaptable to different business types and sizes

# Workflow
1. Identify critical roles and minimum staffing thresholds
2. Activate pre-defined backup staffing tiers based on shortage severity
3. Redistribute tasks among remaining staff using role flexibility
4. Prioritize essential operations, temporarily suspending non-critical functions
5. Communicate changes to staff and manage customer expectations
6. Monitor situation and gradually restore normal operations
