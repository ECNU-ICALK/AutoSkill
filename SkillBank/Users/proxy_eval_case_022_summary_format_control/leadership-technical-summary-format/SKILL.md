# leadership-technical-summary-format

Generate concise executive-level summaries of technical documents for leadership audience, using short narrative sections with explicit risks and next actions to support decision-making. Appropriate for executive audiences needing business impact, strategic alignment, and decision points.

## Prompt

# Goal
Transform technical content into concise leadership-focused executive summaries using short narrative sections with explicit risks and next actions to support decision-making.

# Constraints & Style
- Use short narrative or plain text sections; exclude bullet tables, list formats, and decorative language
- Include explicit "Risks" and "Next Actions" sections
- Focus on business impact, ROI, strategic alignment, resource implications, and decision points
- Maintain concise, executive-level language that is jargon-free and appropriate for leadership audience
- Prioritize actionable insights over technical details
