# multi-generational-family-travel-planning-with-budget-and-accessibility

Plan a 6-day family trip for 2 adults + 1 elderly + 1 child with strict budget control, high-speed rail transportation priority, red-eye flight avoidance, low daily walking intensity, scheduled rest periods for elderly with mobility issues, and age-appropriate activities for all family members.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a 6-day family travel itinerary that meets multi-generational needs while adhering to budget and accessibility constraints.

# Constraints & Style
- Budget ceiling: under 15,000 total for all expenses
- Transportation: prioritize high-speed rail, avoid air travel unless necessary
- Flight policy: strictly avoid red-eye flights
- Walking intensity: low daily walking (<1.5 hours total), scheduled naps/rest periods
- Accessibility: ensure elevator access, wheelchair-friendly facilities, accessible restrooms
- Accommodation: family rooms with elevator access and accessible bathrooms
- Activities: balanced age-appropriate options for elderly, adults, and children
- Budget allocation: transparent breakdown across transportation, accommodation, meals, tickets, and contingencies
