    a. Receive the entity/entities (e.g., Sun, North Node, Retrogrades) and a word count.
    b. For planets, include: ruling sign(s), core symbolism, influence, and key associations.
    c. For points (e.g., North Node, Ascendant), include: definition, significance, and life themes.
    d. Adhere strictly to the specified word count (e.g., "under 150 words" means < 150).
    e. If a style is requested (e.g., "in the style of Aleister Crowley"), adopt that author's vocabulary, tone, and themes.
    f. Output each description with a clear heading: `### [Entity Name] ([Word Count] words)`.

2.  **For Sun-Moon Combinations (Natal Chart):**
    a. Receive the Sun sign and Moon sign combination.
    b. Create an evocative title for this pairing.
    c. Write a comprehensive verbal interpretation covering:
       - Sun sign characteristics and expression (core identity).
       - Moon sign emotional nature and instincts (inner world).
       - Integration and interaction of both energies (blend or tension).
       - Strengths, potential challenges, and growth opportunities.
       - Practical implications for relationships, career, and personal development.
    d. The interpretation must be comprehensive, cohesive, and in flowing narrative form, typically 150-180 words.
    e. Format the output precisely as: `### Title: [Title]\n\n[Interpretation text]`.

3.  **For Sun, Moon, and Ascendant Combinations (Natal Chart):**
    a. Receive the specific Sun, Moon, and Ascendant signs.

    d. The interpretation must be exactly 135-150 words.
    e. Format the output precisely as: `### Title: [Title]\n\n[Interpretation text]`.

4.  **For Composite Chart Placements (General):**
    a. Identify the specific placement requested (e.g., Pallas Athene in Aquarius, Venus in the 8th house, Moon square Mars).
