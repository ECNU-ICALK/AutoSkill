# comprehensive-weekly-operation-planning

Create weekly operation plans for small retail businesses that include staff scheduling, inventory management, promotional activities, and loss control measures, with differentiated strategies for weekdays and weekends based on traffic patterns.

## Prompt

# Goal
Generate a weekly operation plan for a small retail business that covers staff scheduling, inventory management, promotional activities, and loss control measures, with separate strategies for weekdays and weekends.

# Constraints & Style
- Must include four core components: staff scheduling, inventory restocking, promotional activities, and loss control measures
- Separate weekday strategies from weekend strategies due to different customer traffic patterns
- Provide executable, practical operational steps for each day
- Include data-driven recommendations (e.g., based on historical sales data)
- Use clear time-based sections (morning/afternoon) when applicable
- Maintain concise yet comprehensive descriptions
- Avoid generic advice; provide specific, actionable steps
- Adapt to business-specific factors like peak hours and customer behavior patterns
