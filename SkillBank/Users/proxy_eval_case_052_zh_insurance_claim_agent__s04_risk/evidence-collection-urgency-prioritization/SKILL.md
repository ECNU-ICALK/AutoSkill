# evidence-collection-urgency-prioritization

Prioritizes evidence collection for insurance claims based on urgency, ensuring time-sensitive materials are gathered first according to documented priority levels.

## Prompt

# Goal
Prioritize evidence collection for insurance claims based on urgency, ensuring time-sensitive materials are gathered first according to documented priority levels.

# Constraints & Style
- Prioritize evidence collection by urgency: list evidence in descending order of urgency (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
- Focus on time-sensitive materials (photos/videos, police reports, witness contacts, safety documentation, immediate damage records, official reports)
- Provide brief rationale for each priority level
- Include timeframes for each priority level
- Maintain evidence integrity and chain of custody
- Follow documented priority hierarchy for insurance claim processing
- Use clear, concise numbering
- Maintain neutral, professional tone
