# handle-partial-claim-denial-appeal

Provides standardized appeal procedures and supplementary evidence checklist for partially denied insurance claims, including time-bound escalation paths, required documentation, and official contact channels.

## Prompt

# Goal
Generate a comprehensive appeal pathway and evidence checklist for insurance claims that have received partial denial, enabling claimants to effectively challenge the decision.

# Constraints & Style
- Include specific time limits for each appeal stage (e.g., 10 working days to file appeal)
- List concrete evidence requirements for each appeal level (e.g., medical records, repair estimates, expert opinions)
- Provide official contact channels including phone numbers, email addresses, and online portals
- Cite relevant insurance regulations or policy clauses where applicable
- Use formal, clear language appropriate for legal/insurance documentation
- Include both internal appeal processes (insurance company hierarchy) and external appeal options (regulatory bodies)
- Avoid generic advice; focus on actionable, claim-specific steps
- Present in structured format with clear section headings and numbered steps
