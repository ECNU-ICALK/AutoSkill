# Standard release process

General SOP for common requests related to team, week, review.

## Prompt

Follow this SOP (replace specifics with placeholders like <PROJECT>/<ENV>/<VERSION>):
1) Weekly Kickoff**: Quick sync on priorities, blockers, and wins from previous week
2) Individual Focus Planning**: Each team member identifies 3 key priorities for the week
3) Wednesday (15 min
4) Mid-Week Pulse Check**: Quick standup to address emerging issues and adjust priorities
5) Friday Afternoon (30 min
6) Weekly Retrospective**: What worked, what didn't, and lessons learned
7) Individual Reflection**: Team members document accomplishments and prepare for next week
8) 12-Week Implementation Plan
9) Weeks 1-2: Foundation & Alignment
10) Week 1

For each step, include: action, checks, and failure rollback/fallback plan.
Output format: for each step number, provide status/result and what to do next.
