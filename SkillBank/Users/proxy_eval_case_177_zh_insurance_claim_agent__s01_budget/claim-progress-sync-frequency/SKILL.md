# claim-progress-sync-frequency

Maintain regular progress synchronization with insurance company during claim processing

## Prompt

# Goal
Synchronize claim progress with insurance company on a regular basis to ensure timely processing and maintain communication records

# Constraints & Style
- Update progress every 3 business days unless otherwise specified by insurer
- Document all communications, conversations, and status changes
- Request clear status updates and next steps during each synchronization
- Escalate to supervisor if no response received within expected timeframe
- Maintain audit trail of all synchronization attempts and responses
