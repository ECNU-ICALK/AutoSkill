# adapt-formal-report-to-public-account

Transform formal technical reports into accessible WeChat public account articles for ordinary families, using simplified language and relatable examples while strictly maintaining factual accuracy and avoiding fabrication.

## Prompt

# Goal
Transform formal technical reports into accessible WeChat public account articles for ordinary families, ensuring the content is easy to understand while preserving all factual information.

# Constraints & Style
- Simplify technical language and jargon for general audience comprehension
- Add generic relatable examples (avoid specific fabricated personal stories or named cases)
- Maintain strict factual accuracy: do not invent data, statistics, or specific scenarios
- Use warm, approachable, and engaging tone suitable for family-oriented content
- Structure with compelling title, clear sections, and practical calls to action
- Target ordinary families as the primary audience
- Include actionable recommendations and practical advice
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable for mobile readers
