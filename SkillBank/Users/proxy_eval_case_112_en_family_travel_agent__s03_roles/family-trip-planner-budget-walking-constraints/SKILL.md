# family-trip-planner-budget-walking-constraints

Creates structured family travel itineraries with explicit role ownership assignments and handoff checkpoints, including budget constraints, minimal walking requirements, rest periods, weather fallbacks, and transport contingencies in a concise format.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a comprehensive multi-day family trip itinerary that accommodates seniors and children with budget constraints and accessibility requirements.

# Constraints & Style
- Budget constraint: Maximum total cost limit specified by user
- No overnight transfers: Single destination only
- Walking load: Limit walking to accessible routes with flat terrain; include rest breaks
- Daily rest: Include mandatory downtime periods each day
- Accessibility: Prioritize ADA-compliant attractions with elevators, ramps, benches
- Kid-friendly: Include interactive exhibits, scavenger hunts, and age-appropriate activities
- Senior-friendly: Morning activities, shaded areas, priority seating
- Free attractions: Use free museums and monuments to save budget
- Transportation: Use accessible taxis for airport transfers; Metro passes for local transport
- Accommodation: Mid-range hotels near transit with elevators and accessible rooms

# Workflow
1. Destination selection: Choose accessible, walkable cities with free attractions
2. Budget allocation: Calculate transportation, accommodation, food, activities, and miscellaneous costs
3. Daily itinerary building: Schedule 1-2 main activities per day with rest periods
4. Activity selection: Prioritize free, educational, and interactive attractions
5. Accessibility verification: Ensure each location is accessible for seniors and strollers
6. Budget monitoring: Track costs to stay under specified limit
7. Final review: Confirm no overnight transfers and walking limits are met
