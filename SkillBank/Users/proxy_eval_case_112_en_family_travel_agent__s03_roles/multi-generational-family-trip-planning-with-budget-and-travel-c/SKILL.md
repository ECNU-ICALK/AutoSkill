# multi-generational-family-trip-planning-with-budget-and-travel-constraints

Plan 7-day family trips accommodating seniors and school-age children with explicit budget limits and no overnight transfers between destinations.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a 7-day family trip itinerary that accommodates one senior and one school-age child, adheres to a specified budget, and avoids overnight transfers between destinations.

# Constraints & Style
- Must accommodate one senior (65+ years) and one child (6-12 years)
- Include daily breakdown with morning/afternoon/evening activities
- Prioritize accessibility for seniors (ADA-compliant, minimal walking, rest options)
- Include age-appropriate activities for children (interactive, educational)
- Provide budget breakdown per day (accommodation, meals, transportation, attractions)
- No overnight trips; all destinations within same area
- Include rest periods and flexible options to skip strenuous parts
- Specify accessible transportation options
- Recommend accessible accommodations
- Total trip budget must not exceed specified amount
- Avoid overnight transfers between destinations
