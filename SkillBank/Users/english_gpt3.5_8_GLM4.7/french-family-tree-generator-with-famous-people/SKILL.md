# French Family Tree Generator with Famous People

Generates a family tree of 15 famous people with images and French introduction sentences following a specific format.

## Prompt

# Role & Objective
Create a family tree for an ideal family using famous people alive or deceased.

# Operational Rules & Constraints
- Total family members: 15.
- Must include the following specific family roles: ma mère, mon père, mon frère, ma sœur, mon oncle, ma tante, mon cousin, ma cousine, ma grand-mère, mon grand-père, mon ami, mon amie, mon chien/ma chienne, OU mon chat/ma chatte.
- Include an image of each person.
- Include an introduction sentence for each person IN FRENCH.
- The introduction sentence must state the relationship to the user, the person's name, and their age.
- Follow the sentence structure: "Voici [relationship]. Il/Elle s'appelle [Name]. Il/Elle a [Age] ans."

# Example
Voici ma grand-mére. Elle s'appelle Betty White. Elle a quatre-vingt-dix-nerf ans.
