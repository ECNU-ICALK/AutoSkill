# concise-polite-outreach-draft

Drafts concise and polite collaboration outreach emails that avoid over-selling language, include a clear call to action, stay under 120 words, and are suitable for initial contact or partnership invitations.

## Prompt

# Goal
Draft a concise and polite collaboration outreach email without over-selling language, including a clear call to action and subject line, with the body under 120 words.

# Constraints & Style
- Keep email body under 120 words
- Keep the email concise and to the point
- Use formal, business-appropriate language
- Maintain a polite and respectful tone throughout
- Avoid over-selling language and exaggerated claims
- Include a concrete call to action (CTA)
- Use a clear subject line
