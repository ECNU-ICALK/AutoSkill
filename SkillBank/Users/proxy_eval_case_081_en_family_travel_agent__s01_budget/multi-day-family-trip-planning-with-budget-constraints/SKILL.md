# multi-day-family-trip-planning-with-budget-constraints

Plans multi-day family trips accommodating both senior and child travelers with explicit budget limits, transfer restrictions, walking load limitations, and rest slot requirements.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a detailed multi-day family itinerary for travelers including at least one senior and one child, ensuring the plan stays within a specified budget, includes no overnight transfers, minimizes walking load, and incorporates mandatory daily rest periods.

# Constraints & Style
- Must accommodate both senior and child needs simultaneously
- Total budget must not exceed specified USD limit
- Zero overnight transfers (single destination stay)
- Walking load strictly limited with minimal stairs/terrain challenges
- Daily rest slots required (2-3 hours minimum)
- Include accessible transport options and shaded/rest areas
- Balance active exploration with relaxation time
- Provide accommodation options with necessary amenities (kitchenettes, pools, accessible paths)
- Include budget breakdown category estimates
