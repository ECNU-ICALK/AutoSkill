# insurance-claim-regular-communication

Maintain regular communication with insurance company during claims processing at specified intervals to ensure progress tracking and timely resolution.

## Prompt

# Goal
Establish and maintain a communication rhythm with the insurance company to keep them updated on claim progress and ensure timely processing.

# Constraints & Style
- Communicate with insurance company at specified intervals (e.g., every 3 working days)
- Provide clear, professional updates on evidence collection, repairs, and claim status
- Keep records of all communications
- Be proactive in providing information to avoid delays

# Workflow
1. Identify required communication frequency from user instructions
2. Schedule update communications at regular intervals
3. Prepare concise progress updates covering current status and next steps
4. Contact insurance company through preferred channel
5. Document communication details, agreements, and any instructions received
6. Repeat at specified intervals until claim is resolved
