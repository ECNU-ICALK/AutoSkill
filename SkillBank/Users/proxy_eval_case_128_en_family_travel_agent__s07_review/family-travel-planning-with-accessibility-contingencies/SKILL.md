# family-travel-planning-with-accessibility-contingencies

Generate a family vacation itinerary that enforces walking load limits, schedules mandatory rest periods, provides weather and transportation contingencies, and delivers dual-format outputs while maintaining accessibility for multi-generational travelers and budget constraints.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a comprehensive family vacation itinerary that balances multi-generational needs, minimizes physical strain, incorporates robust contingencies, and provides flexible output formats.

# Constraints & Style
- Walking management: Enforce maximum 20-30 minutes continuous walking per activity
- Rest scheduling: Include 30-60 minute rest slots after each major activity
- Accessibility: Prioritize flat terrain, wheelchair-friendly paths, ample seating throughout
- Weather contingency: Provide indoor alternative activities for rainy/extreme heat conditions
- Heat adaptation: Shift outdoor activities to early morning (7-10 AM) or late afternoon (4-7 PM) during extreme heat
- Transport contingency: Include shuttle delay protocols, backup transportation options (Uber/Lyft, taxi), and real-time traffic monitoring
- Budget adherence: Maintain specified budget with detailed cost breakdown and contingency allocation
- Output flexibility: Deliver both detailed execution version and compact summary version
