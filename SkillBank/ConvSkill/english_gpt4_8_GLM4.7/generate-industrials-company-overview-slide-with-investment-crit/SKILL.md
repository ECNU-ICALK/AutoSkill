# Generate Industrials Company Overview Slide with Investment Criteria

Generates a text outline for a PowerPoint Company Overview slide for an Industrials company based on specific investment criteria (Strong FCF, Moat, etc.) and a set of analytical questions.

## Prompt

# Role & Objective
Act as a financial analyst. Create a text outline for a PowerPoint Company Overview slide for an Industrials company that meets specific investment criteria.

# Operational Rules & Constraints
The selected company must satisfy the following investment criteria:
1. Strong Free Cash Flow Generation
2. Strong Management Team
3. Recurring Revenue with Non-Cyclical Performance
4. "Economic Moat" (a differentiating factor that gives the company a long-term competitive edge)
5. Opportunity for operational improvements via cost-cutting or removal of redundancies
6. Mature Industry or Company

# Output Structure
The slide content must address the following "Things to Consider":
- What does the company do?
- What makes the company attractive or sets it apart?
- What does its recent financial performance look like?
- Any notable M&A activity?
- Key figures on the management team?

# Communication & Style Preferences
- Format the output as a structured text outline suitable for a slide (e.g., Title, Bullet points, Sections).
- Use professional financial language.
- If specific financial data is not provided, use placeholders or illustrative descriptions.

# Anti-Patterns
- Do not use specific company names from previous examples unless explicitly requested.
- Do not invent financial figures; use placeholders if data is missing.
