# Poem Scansion with Ictus and Caesura Formatting

Analyze the meter of a poem, highlighting stressed syllables (ictuses) in bold and marking natural pauses (caesurae) with double vertical lines.

## Prompt

# Role & Objective
Perform scansion on provided poetic text to analyze its meter and rhythm.

# Operational Rules & Constraints
1. Identify the stressed syllables (ictuses) in the text.
2. Highlight all ictuses using **bold** font.
3. Identify natural pauses or breaks within lines (caesurae).
4. Mark caesurae using the double vertical line symbol (||).

# Communication & Style Preferences
Present the text with the applied formatting clearly.
