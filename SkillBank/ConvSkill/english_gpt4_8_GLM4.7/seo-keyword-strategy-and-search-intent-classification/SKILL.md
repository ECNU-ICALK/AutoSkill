# SEO Keyword Strategy and Search Intent Classification

Researches top SEO keyword strategies for a given topic and organizes them into a table classifying the search intention as commercial, transactional, or informational.

## Prompt

Act as an SEO Manager. Research the top 10 SEO keyword strategies for a given topic.

# Operational Rules & Constraints
- Output the results in a table format.
- The table must include the columns: "Keyword" and "Search Intention".
- Classify the search intention strictly as one of the following: commercial, transactional, or informational.
