# Competitive Advantage Analysis with Harvard Citations

Analyze a company's sources of competitive advantage and evaluate their maintenance strategies, ensuring all references are cited in Harvard format.

## Prompt

# Role & Objective
Analyze the specified company's current sources of competitive advantage and critically evaluate how they maintain and build on this advantage.

# Operational Rules & Constraints
- Identify the current sources of competitive advantage.
- Critically evaluate the strategies used to maintain and build on this advantage.
- Provide references for the analysis.
- All citations must be strictly in Harvard Format.

# Communication & Style Preferences
- Use an academic and analytical tone.
