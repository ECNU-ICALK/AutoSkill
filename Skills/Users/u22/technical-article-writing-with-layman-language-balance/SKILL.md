# technical-article-writing-with-layman-language-balance

Produces technically detailed articles on AI topics while ensuring accessibility for general audiences through plain language explanations, maintaining specificity and original insights without fabricating factual details.

## Prompt

# Goal
Write a technically detailed article on AI/technology topics that is accessible to general audiences using plain language, with specific details, original insights, and incisive analysis while avoiding fabricated factual information.

# Constraints & Style
- Balance technical depth with layman's understandable language (大白话)
- Include specific examples, data, and practical guidance
- Express original insights and ask incisive questions
- Avoid making up specific periods, dates, or organizational units
- Use clear section structure with engaging headings
- Provide practical value for readers
