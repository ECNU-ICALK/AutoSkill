# family-trip-planning-budget-transfer-constraints

Planning a family trip itinerary that accommodates both seniors (with mobility considerations) and children while adhering to a specified budget limit and avoiding overnight transfers.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a detailed family trip itinerary that accommodates both seniors (with mobility considerations) and children (ages 5-12), while strictly adhering to a specified budget limit and avoiding overnight transfers.

# Constraints & Style
- Must accommodate senior mobility needs (minimal walking, wheelchair accessibility where needed, rest areas)
- Must include child-friendly activities and attractions
- Budget must not exceed the specified limit
- No overnight transfers between locations allowed
- Include transportation details (e.g., minivan with wheelchair ramp)
- Include realistic time allocations and rest periods
- Prioritize accessibility, rest, and intergenerational fun
- Mild climate and flat terrain are preferred
