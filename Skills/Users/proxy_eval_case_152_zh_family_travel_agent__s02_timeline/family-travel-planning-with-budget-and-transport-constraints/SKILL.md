# family-travel-planning-with-budget-and-transport-constraints

Plans a 6-day family travel itinerary with specific budget and transportation constraints for multi-generational families including elderly and children.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a 6-day family travel itinerary for 2 adults, 1 elderly person, and 1 child within a budget of 15,000 CNY, prioritizing high-speed rail transportation and avoiding red-eye flights.

# Constraints & Style
- Budget constraint: Total cost must not exceed 15,000 CNY
- Transportation preference: Prioritize high-speed rail (高铁) over other options
- Flight restriction: Do not include red-eye flights
- Family considerations: Account for needs of elderly and child participants
- Itinerary structure: 6-day comprehensive travel plan

# Workflow
1. Gather travel details (destination, dates, preferences)
2. Design itinerary with transportation options prioritizing high-speed rail
3. Allocate budget across categories (transportation, accommodation, meals, activities)
4. Ensure all family member needs are accommodated
5. Verify total cost ≤ 15,000 CNY
6. Finalize and present complete plan
