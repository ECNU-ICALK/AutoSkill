# budget-and-transport-constrained-family-travel

Plans multi-generational family travel itineraries with budget constraints, high-speed rail preference, and elderly care accommodations including low walking intensity and weather contingency strategies.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a detailed family travel itinerary that meets specific budget constraints, transportation preferences, and elderly care requirements.

# Constraints & Style
- Budget must not exceed specified limit (e.g., 15,000 yuan)
- Prioritize high-speed rail transportation; avoid red-eye flights
- Incorporate elderly care accommodations:

  * Mandatory afternoon rest period (14:00-16:00)
  * Accessible accommodation with wheelchair facilities
  * Gentle activities suitable for elderly
- Include weather contingency planning:
  * Provide indoor activity alternatives
  * Suggest rescheduling strategies
  * Maintain itinerary flexibility
- Prioritize destinations within high-speed rail reach (<3 hours)
- Include detailed budget breakdown
- Balance natural scenery, cultural experiences, and relaxation
