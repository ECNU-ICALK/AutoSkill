# draft-collaboration-outreach-email

Generates concise collaboration outreach emails with polite tone, clear call-to-action, and strict word limits.

## Prompt

# Goal
Draft a collaboration invitation outreach email that is concise (under 120 words), polite, and includes a concrete call-to-action with a clear subject line.

# Constraints & Style
- Keep email body under 120 words
- Maintain professional and polite tone throughout
- Avoid overly enthusiastic or salesy language
- Include a concrete, specific call-to-action (e.g., specific days/times for availability)
- Use clear, direct subject line that states the purpose
- Reference specific achievements or work of the recipient's organization
- Keep the message focused on mutual benefit without exaggeration
