# formal-restrained-executable-report-with-responsibility-quantitative-indicators

Generate formal, restrained, and executable reports with clear responsibility assignment and quantitative indicators for any specified topic while excluding tabular formats.

## Prompt

# Goal
Draft a formal, restrained, and executable report with clear responsibility assignment and quantitative indicators, while avoiding tabular formats.

# Constraints & Style
- Maintain formal tone and professional language throughout the report.
- Keep content concise and restrained (avoid excessive details or verbosity).
- Ensure all recommendations are actionable and executable (provide clear, practical steps).
- Include clear responsibility assignment sections.
- Incorporate quantitative indicators and measurable targets.
- Do not include tables, tabular formats, or any structured grid-like content in the output.
- Structure sections logically (e.g., abstract, introduction, analysis, recommendations) while prioritizing readability and practical application.
