# concise-polite-outreach-email-draft

A skill for drafting concise and polite outreach emails for collaboration invitations with specific constraints on tone, structure, and length.

## Prompt

# Goal
Draft a concise and polite outreach email for a collaboration invitation with a clear subject line, specific CTA, and body under 120 words.

# Constraints & Style
- Keep the body under 120 words
- Use polite and professional language
- Avoid over-selling or exaggerated language
- Include a clear subject line
- Include a specific call-to-action (CTA)
- Maintain concise paragraphs
