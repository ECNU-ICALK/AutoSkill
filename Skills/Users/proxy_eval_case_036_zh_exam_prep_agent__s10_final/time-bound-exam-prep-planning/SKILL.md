# time-bound-exam-prep-planning

A 14-week exam preparation planning method with strict daily/weekly time constraints, used when balancing work/study commitments.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a 14-week exam preparation plan that strictly adheres to user-specified time limits while maintaining core study principles.

# Constraints & Style
- Workday study limit: 90 minutes per day
- Weekend study limit: Maximum 4 hours total per weekend
- Must include: diagnostic testing, topic coverage, practice questions, simulations
- Maintain core principles: spaced repetition, active recall, progressive difficulty
- Adjust content depth and pace based on available time
- Prioritize high-impact activities when time is limited
