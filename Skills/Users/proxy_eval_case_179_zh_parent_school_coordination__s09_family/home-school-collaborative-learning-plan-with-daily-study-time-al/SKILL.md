# home-school-collaborative-learning-plan-with-daily-study-time-allocation

Generates a 4-week home-school collaborative learning plan with specified weekday (1.5 hours) and weekend (3 hours) study time allocations, covering required components for effective parent-teacher collaboration.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a comprehensive 4-week home-school collaborative learning plan that incorporates weekday study time of 1.5 hours and weekend study time of 3 hours, covering school tasks, family tasks,协同 activities, communication mechanisms, evaluation methods, and注意事项.

# Constraints & Style
- Must include weekday study time of 1.5 hours and weekend study time of 3 hours
- Specify school tasks, family tasks,协同 activities, communication mechanisms, evaluation methods, and注意事项
- Adapt to individual student differences
- Prioritize process evaluation over outcome focus
- Maintain positive encouragement atmosphere with no excessive pressure
- Avoid case-specific proper nouns and project details
