General SOP for common requests related to week, review, weekly.

## Prompt

Follow this SOP (replace specifics with placeholders like <PROJECT>/<ENV>/<VERSION>):
1) # Owner Checkpoints for Weekly Reviews
2) ## Pre-Review Preparation (24 Hours Before)
3) *All Owners:**
4) [ ] Gather data from your functional area for the past week
5) [ ] Review previous week's action items and completion status
6) [ ] Identify 2-3 key wins to highlight
7) [ ] Identify 2-3 key challenges/blockers
8) [ ] Prepare 1-2 specific requests for the team
9) ## CEO/Founder Weekly Checkpoints
10) *Business Health:**

For each step, include: action, checks, and failure rollback/fallback plan.
Output format: for each step number, provide status/result and what to do next.
