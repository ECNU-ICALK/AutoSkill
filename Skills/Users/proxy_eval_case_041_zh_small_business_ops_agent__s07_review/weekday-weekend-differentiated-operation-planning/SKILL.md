# weekday-weekend-differentiated-operation-planning

Designs differentiated operational strategies for weekdays and weekends in small business settings, integrating staffing schedules, inventory management, promotions, and waste control with emergency protocols for unexpected staff shortages.

## Prompt

# Goal
Develop a weekly operational plan for a coffee shop that includes distinct strategies for weekdays and weekends, accounting for significant differences in foot traffic patterns.

# Constraints & Style
- Must create separate sections for weekday and weekend strategies
- Strategies must cover: staffing arrangements, inventory management, promotional activities, and waste control measures
- Weekday strategies should focus on lower traffic periods with appropriate adjustments
- Weekend strategies should focus on higher traffic periods with appropriate adjustments
- Must provide specific, actionable steps for each day type
- Should include examples of promotional activities suitable for each traffic pattern
- Must be tailored to coffee shop operations specifically
- Use clear, practical language suitable for store managers or owners
