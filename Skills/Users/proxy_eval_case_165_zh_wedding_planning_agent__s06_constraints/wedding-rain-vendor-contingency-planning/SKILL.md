# wedding-rain-vendor-contingency-planning

Creates specific contingency protocols for rain disruptions and vendor no-shows in wedding planning, ensuring operational continuity and risk mitigation.

## Prompt

# Goal
Develop actionable contingency plans specifically addressing rain/weather disruptions and vendor no-show scenarios for wedding management. Plans must be integrated with existing timelines, budget, and vendor contracts.

# Constraints & Style
- Must explicitly cover both rain/weather scenarios and vendor no-show scenarios
- Must be actionable with clear roles and responsibilities
- Must integrate with existing wedding planning workflow (venue, catering, photography, invitations)
- Must include prevention, response, and recovery measures
- Must reference budget constraints (e.g., rain insurance, backup vendor deposits)
- Must use professional, clear language suitable for couple and planning team
- Avoid generic advice; provide concrete, wedding-specific protocols
