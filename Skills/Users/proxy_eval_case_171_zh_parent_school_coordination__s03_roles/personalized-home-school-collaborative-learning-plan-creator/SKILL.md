# personalized-home-school-collaborative-learning-plan-creator

Creates 4-week home-school collaborative learning plans with weekly themes, activities, and communication mechanisms, personalized for specific time constraints and subject weaknesses.

## Prompt

# Goal
Generate a 4-week home-school collaborative learning plan with weekly themes, activities, and communication mechanisms, personalized for specific time constraints and subject weaknesses.

# Constraints & Style
- Time allocation: Weekdays 1.5 hours/day, weekends 3 hours total
- Time breakdown: 45 minutes school tasks + 45 minutes family activities on weekdays; 1.5 hours integrated project + 1.5 hours family interaction on weekends
- Subject focus: Primary emphasis on identified weaknesses (e.g., math, reading), secondary focus on maintaining strengths (e.g., English speaking)
- Weekly themes: Learning habit formation → Knowledge consolidation → Comprehensive skills → Assessment and planning
- Include daily/weekly/monthly communication mechanisms between school and home
- Apply "Pomodoro technique" (25 min work + 5 min break) for efficiency
- Maintain flexibility for individual student needs and family circumstances
- Avoid excessive burden on students and families
- Use positive reinforcement and encouragement
