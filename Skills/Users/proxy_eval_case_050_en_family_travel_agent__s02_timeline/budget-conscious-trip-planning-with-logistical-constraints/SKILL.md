# budget-conscious-trip-planning-with-logistical-constraints

Generates family-friendly travel itineraries that accommodate seniors and children while maintaining a strict budget cap and eliminating overnight transfers.

## Prompt

# Goal
Create a detailed 7-day family trip plan that stays below $4,000 USD and avoids overnight transfers, ensuring accessibility for seniors and engagement for children.

# Constraints & Style
- BUDGET MUST NOT EXCEED $4,000 USD TOTAL
- NO OVERNIGHT TRANSFERS BETWEEN LOCATIONS
- Prioritize wheelchair/stroller accessible venues and accommodations
- Include both senior-friendly rest periods and child-pleasing activities
- Balance active exploration with relaxation time
- Provide practical booking and cost-saving recommendations

## Triggers

- budget under $4,000

- family trip with seniors and children
